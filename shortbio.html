Ella Phillips Student

As a rising senior at the University of Iowa studying economic policy with minors in GIS and urban studies, I am interested in building better communities through policy and infrastructure. I believe that equity, environmentalism, the economy, and interconnectedness of individuals within communities are shaped by our surroundings. My current goal is to work in a position that allows me to work within the framework of policy to create communities that nurture the people who live and work there no matter the size, demographic, or economic status of the city/town. I aim to further my education in applied policy related to planning and become a more qualified candidate to work within or alongside the local government with the goal to curate cities that benefit all who live there while protecting the environment.
